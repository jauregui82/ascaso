High-quality materials and components
The Dream coffee machine is manufactured in aluminium. After preliminary polishing, the machine is treated with a high-quality varnish (the same coating as used in Formula 1 and on boats), with a range of 15 colours and three finishes: Gloss, matte and hand-crafted (see colours).

As well as the wide range of colours, we also offer our mirror-polish finish, featuring an artisan-mechanical process that gives its own character to each piece (enlarge).

Tam-tamper
The Dream model (ground coffee/versatile) includes a Tam-tamper: Professional aluminium coffee tamper (57 mm) designed by Marc Aranyó.
Aluminium
Hand-polished
This finish is achieved by polishing each part by hand, one by one.
Any irregularities on the polished surface are the result of this laborious hand-crafted process that makes each piece unique.

See video
Special cappuccino steam wand
A special feature which comes as standard on all our products. The design of the steam wand allows the user to make creamy cappuccinos and lattes with an ease that seems like magic.
For real purists who prefer the professional barista concept, there is an optional nozzle with a central outlet.
See steam wand for Pid models.
 
Powerful steam/hot water
The new aluminium + stainless steel group guarantees continuous, unlimited steam. The life of the piece is now much longer thanks to the fact that lime scale does not build up on the stainless steel circuit.
 
1.3-litre water reservoir
Can be removed for easy maintenance, cleaning and visual access, allowing the user to quickly check the amount of water in the chamber. The reservoir can be filled with the coffee machine in operation.
Cup warmer
Emulating professional coffee bar machines, the upper part features a surface that keeps cups warm, ensuring that coffee remains at its optimum temperature.



Wire tray
As well as the decorative aspect, the wire tray keeps the base of the cup free of coffee residue. Easy to clean.


Stainless steel tray
Easily removed for cleaning.
 
Metal switches
The high quality of the machine makes it a pleasure to use. Emphasises the retro look.
